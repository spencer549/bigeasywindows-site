Our Story

We started Big Easy Windows because we were tired of seeing New Orleans homeowners get burned by out-of-town contractors and big-box franchises.

Too many companies show up late (or not at all), quote one price and charge another, and send random subcontractors who don't know a shotgun house from a Creole cottage.

We knew there had to be a better way. So we built a window company around three simple promises:

  • Show up when we say we will. No "between 8 and 5" windows. We give you a time, we keep it.
  • Quote it before we do it. No hidden fees. The price we quote is the price you pay.
  • Local crew, not subcontractors. Our team, our training, our standards.

That's it. Just honest work from people who live here, know the weather, and treat your home like our own.

Why Local Matters

New Orleans isn't like other cities. Our homes are unique: historic shotguns, raised Creole cottages, modern builds in Lakeview. Our weather is unique, too: hurricanes, humidity, and heat that test every window we install.

A national franchise reading from a script doesn't understand that. We do.

We live here. We've weathered the storms. We know which windows hold up and which don't. And when the next big one comes through, we're not flying in from out of state. We're already here, ready to help our neighbors.

That's what local means to us.
